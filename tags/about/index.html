About Ana

Ana Sun (pronounced “Soon”) writes from the edge of an ancient town along the River Ouse in the south-east of England.
She spent her childhood in Malaysian Borneo, and has lived on two other islands prior to moving to the UK.
In another life, she might have been a musician, an anthropologist—or a botanist obsessed with edible flowers.

Say hello via email or on Mastodon at @ana@wandering.shop.
