Soul Noodles

The shadows on the faded wall danced silently to a rhythm that I could never put my finger on. Every time I stayed late to finish the day’s tasks, these silhouettes hypnotised me with their slither, lulling me into the sedate sleep-spell…

Ana Sun (pronounced “Soon”) writes from the edge of an ancient town in the south-east of England. She spent her childhood in Malaysian Borneo and grew up living on islands. One of her earliest Solarpunk stories, "Dandelion Brew", earned her a Utopia Award nomination. "The Scent of Green" was featured in The Best of British Science Fiction 2023. In another life, she might have been a musician, an anthropologist—or a botanist obsessed with edible flowers.
